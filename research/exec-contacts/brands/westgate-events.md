## Suggested first-touch sequence
1. **LinkedIn DM** with a 2-line hook referencing a specific deal of theirs we currently rank #1 for.
2. **Cold email** 48h later if no LinkedIn response, repeating the hook + one-line pitch ("we have Westgate Events traffic — would you like the audit").
3. **Phone call** to 888-215-2022, ask reception for "marketing" or "affiliates", reference your previous outreach.
