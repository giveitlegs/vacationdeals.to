## Email patterns to try once a name is identified
Common patterns at corporate domains: `firstname.lastname@`, `flastname@`, `firstinitiallastname@`. Verify with [Hunter.io](https://hunter.io/) or [RocketReach](https://rocketreach.co/) before sending.

## Suggested first-touch sequence
1. **LinkedIn DM** with a 2-line hook referencing a specific deal of theirs we currently rank #1 for.
2. **Cold email** 48h later if no LinkedIn response, repeating the hook + one-line pitch ("we have Divi Resorts traffic — would you like the audit").
3. **Phone call** to main switchboard, ask reception for "marketing" or "affiliates", reference your previous outreach.

## Hooks they care about
- We rank #1 for several of their brand-name + city queries (we do).
- We can show them which affiliates send junk traffic (Reality Index data).
- Banner placement at top of their highest-traffic landing page on our site.
- Free first month of placement to validate fit.
