# 5 Weird & Viral Lead-Gen Features

Catchy, counterculture features that follow the Resort Roulette blueprint:
**engagement hook → email capture → vacpack conversion**.

All are lightweight, meme-able, and highly shareable.

---

## 1. Timeshare Presentation Survival Kit 🎯

**Concept:** Interactive quiz/game. 20 scenarios that actually happen during timeshare presentations. User picks response. Score at end reveals "Survival Level" from Pushover → Ice Cold.

**Sample scenarios:**
- "They offer you $200 in free food credit if you stay an extra hour. You..."
- "The sales manager comes in with 'a one-time-only deal just for you.' You..."
- "They ask 'Don't you want the BEST for your family?' You..."
- "They show you the 'VIP tier' that's 'only available today.' You..."

**Viral hook:** Shareable score cards ("I'm 87% Ice Cold. Can you beat me?")
**Lead capture:** Email to get "The Official Timeshare Pushback Phrase Book PDF" (downloadable guide with real-world exit phrases)
**SEO angle:** "how to say no at timeshare presentation", "timeshare presentation tips"
**URL:** `/survival-kit`

---

## 2. VacPack Bingo 🎱

**Concept:** Digital + printable bingo card with timeshare presentation tropes. Check off as they happen during a real presentation.

**Sample squares:**
- "Free breakfast mentioned"
- "Manager 'sharpens the pencil' on price"
- "Shown the 'exclusive members-only' section"
- "Told about property's appreciation in value"
- "Offered 'priority booking' as bonus"
- "Mentioned Bahamas cruise"
- "Emotional story about grandkids"
- "Shown photos of empty resort at 'peak season'"
- "Said 'this is a once-in-a-lifetime price'"
- "Offered $50 Amex gift card to stay longer"

**Viral hook:** "BINGO!" share button screenshots to social media
**Lead capture:** Submit completed card → win discount code on next vacpack
**SEO angle:** "timeshare presentation bingo", "timeshare sales tactics"
**URL:** `/vacpack-bingo`

**Bonus:** Gamification — users who complete 3+ bingos get "Certified Timeshare Survivor" badge on their profile

---

## 3. The $59 Challenge 💰

**Concept:** Community challenge. Users plan + execute a full 3-night vacation for under $200 total (including a $59 vacpack). Submit their itinerary with photos. Community votes. Winners get featured + prize.

**Structure:**
- Submission form: destination, vacpack used, food costs, activities, total spent
- Photo requirement (proof)
- Public vote (upvote/downvote)
- Monthly winner featured on homepage
- Prize: $100 toward next vacpack

**Viral hook:** "#The59Challenge" user-generated content that becomes free marketing. Watch influencers make content about it.
**Lead capture:** Account required to submit (email capture)
**SEO angle:** "cheap vacation under 200", "budget vacation challenge", "how to vacation on $59"
**URL:** `/59-challenge`

**Bonus:** Featured winners become testimonials on deal pages — powerful social proof

---

## 4. VacPack Time Machine 🕰️

**Concept:** Historical pricing slider. Drag across months/years to see how Orlando vacpack prices changed. "This week in April 2024, Orlando was $89. In 2025, it was $99. In 2026, it's $149. Predict 2027..."

**Features:**
- Interactive timeline slider (D3.js or lightweight custom)
- Shows price trend per destination
- Overlays events ("Spring Break 2026 peak")
- "What's the record low?" leaderboard
- Predict-the-future game: users guess next year's price

**Viral hook:** "Best deal of all time at [destination]" — shareable cards
**Lead capture:** Email signup to get "Weekly Deal Alert when prices hit historical lows"
**SEO angle:** "orlando vacation price history", "when cheapest time to book orlando"
**URL:** `/vacpack-time-machine`

**Bonus:** Uses our existing 40,939 price observations — zero new data collection needed. Pure value from existing data.

---

## 5. The Deal Scratch-Off 🎟️

**Concept:** Digital scratch-off card. User clicks to "scratch" with mouse/finger. Reveals a RANDOM mystery deal with surprise elements ("You revealed: $89 Orlando + FREE park tickets!")

**Mechanics:**
- Daily limit (1 free scratch per day per session)
- Additional scratches via email signup or referral
- Scratch animation with satisfying reveal
- Confetti + sound on reveal
- Rare "GOLDEN TICKET" scratch (1-in-100) unlocks premium deal tier

**Viral hook:** FOMO-driven — users check back daily just to scratch
**Lead capture:** Out of scratches → email for 5 more
**SEO angle:** "mystery vacation deals", "surprise vacation package"
**URL:** `/scratch-and-win`

**Bonus:** Works on mobile (touch drag), perfect for TikTok/Reels videos ("POV: you scratched off a $59 Vegas trip")

## Combined Strategy

**Ideal launch sequence:**
1. **Survival Kit** (first) — content-heavy, pure SEO play, zero dependencies
2. **Time Machine** (second) — uses existing data, high value/low effort
3. **Bingo** (third) — viral potential, shareable
4. **Scratch-Off** (fourth) — high daily engagement
5. **$59 Challenge** (fifth) — requires UGC moderation, launch when admin CMS is mature enough to handle submissions

**Cross-feature synergies:**
- All 5 feed into the same email list
- Bingo + Survival Kit share the "tactics" content library
- Time Machine data powers Rate Recap Showdown
- Scratch-Off winners get featured on landing pages

**KPIs per feature:**
- Email captures per 1,000 visitors
- Time on page
- Share rate (organic social)
- Click-through to actual deals
- Return visitor rate
